Real-World Impact

实际用途

  • Recovery for Post-Chemotherapy/Radiation Patients: Accelerate recovery and restore vitality by supporting the body’s natural healing processes.
    用于化疗/放疗后患者的恢复： 通过支持身体的自然恢复过程，加快康复并重获活力。
  • Immunodeficiency Support and Overall Wellness: Boost immune function and promote long-term health. Perfect for the elderly, children, and individuals with weakened immunity.
    免疫缺陷支持及整体健康： 增强免疫功能，促进长期健康。特别适合老年人、儿童及免疫力较弱的人群。
  • Athletic Performance Enhancement: Enhance endurance, reduce recovery time, and promote physical resilience for athletes.
    提升运动表现： 提高耐力，缩短恢复时间，并增强运动员的身体韧性。
  • Stress Reduction and Mental Clarity: Support stress management and improve focus and mental clarity.
    缓解压力与提升专注力： 帮助管理压力，提高专注力和清晰度。
  • Anti-Aging Benefits: Slow down the aging process by supporting cellular health and vitality.
    抗衰老功效： 通过支持细胞健康和活力，延缓衰老过程。
  • Support for Autoimmune Conditions: Modulate immune responses and reduce inflammation for individuals with autoimmune disorders.
    自体免疫疾病的支持： 调节免疫反应，减少炎症，帮助自体免疫疾病患者缓解症状。

    慢性病的辅助治疗： 辅助慢性疾病治疗，有助于提高生活质量。
  • General Health Maintenance: Promote immune balance and overall wellness for healthy individuals.
    日常健康维护： 帮助健康人群维持免疫平衡和整体健康。
